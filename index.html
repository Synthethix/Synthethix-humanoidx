SYNTHETIX

An overnight Hackathon at VIT Chennai, pushing innovation and creativity to the limits.

About

Synthetix is the premier overnight hackathon organized by HumanoidX, the leading tech innovation club of VIT Chennai. As our flagship event, Synthetix brings together brilliant minds, passionate developers, and creative problem-solvers for an electrifying experience that pushes the boundaries of technology.


With cutting-edge problem statements spanning programming, electrical engineering, and CAD design, Synthetix is the ultimate test of skill, creativity, and endurance..


Are you ready to innovate, build, and disrupt? Join us at Synthetix and be part of the future of technology!

Timeline

Day 1:

8:00 am Arrival & Orientation

Participants arrive at VIT Chennai, register, and settle in. Following registration, there will be an orientation session covering the hackathon rules, schedule, available resources, and judging criteria.

Day 1:

10:00 am Hacking Begins

The hackathon officially begins! Problem statements are released, and teams dive into crafting their groundbreaking solutions. Let the innovation begin! Mentors will be available to provide guidance and assistance throughout the night.

Day 1:

1:00 pm to 2:00 pm Lunch Break

Lunch break is here! Time to recharge, refuel, and strategize before diving back into the competition. Enjoy your meal!

Day 1:

5:00 pm Refreshments

Refreshments will be provided! Take a break, grab a snack, and fuel up for the next round of innovation. Enjoy!

Day 1:

5:30 pm Review 1

Review 1 begins! Judges assess progress, provide feedback, and guide teams toward refining their innovations. Stay sharp!

Day 1:

6:30 pm to 8:00 pm Dinner time(girls)

Dinner time for girls! Enjoy a delicious meal, relax, and recharge before the next phase of the competition. Note that girls are not allowed outside the venue after 8 pm, therefore make sure you come back to the venue before 8 pm.

Day 1:

7:30 pm to 9:00 pm Dinner time(boys)

Dinner time for boys! Enjoy a delicious meal, relax, and recharge before the next phase of the competition. Note that no one is allowed outside the venue after 9 pm, therefore make sure you come back to the venue before 9 pm.

Day 2:

7:00 am to 9:00 am Freshen-up and Breakfast time

Morning freshen-up and breakfast time! Start your day right—refresh, recharge, and fuel up for the exciting challenges ahead..

Day 2:

10:00 am to 11:30 am

Teams will present their progress to mentors and judges. This is a crucial opportunity to receive feedback, refine your approach, and ensure you're on track to meet the final presentation requirements.

Day 2:

1:00 pm to 2:00 pm Lunch Break

Lunch break is here! Time to recharge, refuel, and strategize before diving back into the competition. Enjoy your meal!

Day 2:

3:00 pm to 3:30 pm Final Presentation

Teams present their final projects to the judges. Presentations should be concise, compelling, and clearly demonstrate the problem your project solves, the solution you've implemented, and its potential impact.

Day 2:

4:30 pm Awards Ceremony

Winners are announced and prizes are awarded. Celebrate your hard work, network with other participants, and enjoy the culmination of the overnight coding marathon!
